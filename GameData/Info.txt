◎SetUp
	Please install Dxlib_VC in C-Drive.
	〇Connect
		Please check thing that is connected two PCs with LAN-Cable.
◎Method of operation
　　　１．Cace Two PCs
　　　　　Operation do only clicking or touching.

　　　　　Player Move：　　　　　slide while clicking
	　Player Wait：　　　　　long click
	　Player Attack：　　　　double click
	　Icon：　		 click each icons
	　Use Item:		 click item-icon and choose action
　	　Alchemy：		 choose two items and click furnace

	  *Item Action
		・使う		use item
		・投げる	throw item far away
		・食べる	eat item
		・装備		equipment item
		・捨てる	throw item in player place
		・拾う		pick up item
		・交換		exchange item

　　　２．Case One PCs
	  Player Move：　　　　　push NumKey1〜4、6〜9
	　Player Wait：　　　　　push NumKey5
	　Player Attack：　　　　push SpaceKey
　　　　　＊Can do PlayerAction.
